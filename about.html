About ZenMarket

ZenMarket is Sri Lanka's premium online marketplace, founded with a mission to connect quality products with discerning customers across the island. We curate the finest electronics, fashion, home goods, and authentic Ceylon products.

Our Mission

To provide Sri Lankans with access to premium products, exceptional service, and a seamless shopping experience — all from the comfort of their homes.

  • Secure PayHere payments
  • 7-day return guarantee

Our Story

Started in Colombo in 2023, ZenMarket has grown to serve customers in all 25 districts of Sri Lanka. Our team of passionate professionals is dedicated to bringing you the best shopping experience in the country.
