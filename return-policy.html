Return & Refund Policy

Return Window

You may return eligible items within 7 days of delivery.

Eligible Items

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags intact
  • Must include proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods (food, spices)
  • Intimate or hygiene products
  • Digital products or downloaded content
  • Items marked 'Final Sale'

Return Process

1. Contact us via WhatsApp (+94 77 123 4567) within 7 days
2. Share your order number and reason for return
3. We'll arrange pickup or provide drop-off instructions
4. Refund processed within 5–7 business days after receiving the item

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. COD orders receive a bank transfer or store credit.
