Shipping Policy

Delivery Coverage

We deliver to all 25 districts in Sri Lanka.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Colombo District: 1–2 business days
  • Western Province (other): 2–3 business days
  • Central, Southern, NW, NC, Sabaragamuwa: 2–4 business days
  • Northern, Eastern, Uva: 3–5 business days

Shipping Rates

  • Colombo: Rs. 250
  • Western Province: Rs. 350–450
  • Other provinces: Rs. 450–600
  • Free shipping on orders over Rs. 5,000

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2:00 PM on business days are processed same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends are processed the next business day.
